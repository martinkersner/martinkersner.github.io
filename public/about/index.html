Co-Founder & CTO at Bisonai

I am the Co-Founder and CTO at Bisonai. Previously, as a Machine Learning Engineer at Hyperconnect, I specialized in mobile machine learning and high-performance model training. My work centered on model and inference engine optimization, low-bit inference, and accelerating training across multi-GPU setups, while building systems to prevent quality shift over time.

Since 2022, I have focused on crypto infrastructure at Bisonai. I spearheaded Orakl Network, the official blockchain oracle for the Kaia blockchain, and DataMaxi+, which processes massive datasets from over 20 exchanges to provide real-time insights across all trading pairs.

Lately, I have been focused on algorithmic crypto trading, specifically market making in AMMs and hybrid arbitrage systems across perpetual markets. I am currently building a system that relies on high-frequency trading and market microstructure, with a growing interest in the application of these principles to prediction markets.
