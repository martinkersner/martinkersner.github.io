<p><strong>Co-Founder &amp; CTO at Bisonai</strong></p>

<p>
  I am the Co-Founder and CTO at <a href="https://bisonai.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Bisonai</a>. Previously, as a Machine Learning Engineer at Hyperconnect, I specialized in mobile machine learning and high-performance model training. My work centered on model and inference engine optimization, low-bit inference, and accelerating training across multi-GPU setups, while building systems to prevent quality shift over time.
</p>

<p>
  Since 2022, I have focused on crypto infrastructure at Bisonai. I spearheaded <a href="https://orakl.network" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Orakl Network</a>, the official blockchain oracle for the Kaia blockchain, and <a href="https://datamaxiplus.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener">DataMaxi+</a>, which processes massive datasets from over 20 exchanges to provide real-time insights across all trading pairs.
</p>

<p>
  Lately, I have been focused on algorithmic crypto trading, specifically market making in AMMs and hybrid arbitrage systems across perpetual markets. I am currently building a system that relies on high-frequency trading and market microstructure, with a growing interest in the application of these principles to prediction markets.
</p>
